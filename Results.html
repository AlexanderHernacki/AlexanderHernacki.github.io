Testing and Results

In order to validate the thermal properties of the selected, aluminium, heat sink, fan, and thermal connection methods a test fixture was fasted, allowing me to directly connect the heat sink to a heater element via an aluminium block. This setup preformed as expected with a total thermal resisistivity of 3.1 W/K and a heat sink thermal resistivity of 2.9 W/K. These values were obtained by measuring ambient and heat sink base temperatures while running the heater at powers of 20-40 Watts and evaluating the steady state heat sink temps.
